Story and lore of the game

Signalis is set in a time where space travel exists.

In this world gestalts also exist, which is a fancy term for androids.

You play as Elster, an LSTR unit (haha funny), on a self-imposed quest to find a girl in photo.

Great holes secretly are digged
where earth's pores ought to suffice,
and things have learnt to walk
that ought to crawl.

That's about it, the rest is completely incomprehensible

It's a beautifully told story but, good luck trying to discipher any meaning behind anything that happens.

I mean just look at the quote in the middle of the screen and the art in the background.

Only thing I got, was that there was a war at some point, and bioresonants are thing.

Bioresonants being people with the ability to control the wavelength of the universe or something.

It is the most lovecraftian videogame to ever exist.

Gameplay

The gameplay is your classic Resident Evil 2 survival horror style game. With a lot of cool point and click puzzles to solve. And from a top-down perspective.

The survival horror aspect is extremely apparent (if you're playing on hard or something) with the very limited resources you can collect and use.

And the puzzles are just puzzles, but quite unique and harder than most games.

The combat isn't that hardest in the world, but it's surprisingly easy to miss, making you line up shots for far longer than you should, since every shot counts

Also enemies get back up, and you don't have a lot of resources for getting rid of their bodies, so good luck with that.

That is all, you are now legally required to play Signalis or you will experience spontaneous combustion
